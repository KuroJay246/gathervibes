# Event Day Guide

## Before guests arrive

1. Confirm the correct Working Event is selected.
2. Review the `Overview` event-day actions.
3. Confirm ticket codes are ready in `Tickets`.
4. Confirm the `Check-In` workflow is ready.
5. Review urgent contacts and the event-day timeline.
6. Keep `Operations` ready for real event-level expenses or issues only.

## During the event

Use these routes most often:

- `Overview` for the event summary, current time, open tasks, and quick actions
- `Check-In` for guest lookup and attendance
- `Tickets` for ticket lookup and assignment questions
- `Operations` for urgent contacts, commitments, and event-level money notes
- `Reports` for read-only follow-up and summary checks

## After the event

1. Mark the event `Completed` when event-day work is over.
2. Review `Reports` for final registration, attendance, and Operations totals.
3. Review unpaid commitments and baker payments in `Operations`.
4. Keep audit logs intact.
5. Archive the event only when the organizer is ready.

## Important boundaries

- `Check-In` records real attendance only.
- Registration payments stay in `Payments`.
- Event-level expenses and commitments stay in `Operations`.
- Approximate historical attendance must not be entered as live system check-ins.
