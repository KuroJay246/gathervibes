# Known Limitations

- Message Builder is copy-only and does not send email, SMS, WhatsApp, or social posts.
- The prompt helper does not call a live AI service.
- Google Sheets OAuth is not active; use CSV export and preview-first import.
- Final event profit is not calculated automatically.
- Registration payments and Operations remain separate record sets by design.
- Public attendee, vendor, sponsor, or partner portals are not active.
- Payment gateway integration is not active.
- CPB reconciliation changes remain evidence-gated and approval-gated.
- Staff access management remains intentionally narrow and safety-first.
