# Baker Payment Guide

## What this guide covers

Use this guide when the organizer needs to review or settle baker commitments without reopening patron payment history.

## Where baker payments live

Baker records belong to event-level commitments in `Operations`. They are separate from registration payments in `Payments`.

Each baker record can include:

- baker name
- company or role
- agreed amount
- amount paid
- balance
- due date
- payment date
- payment method
- evidence
- notes
- current status

## Mark a baker payment as paid

1. Open `Operations` for the correct Working Event.
2. Find the baker record.
3. Confirm the real payment has been made.
4. Update the amount paid, payment method, payment date, and evidence details that are actually known.
5. Confirm the remaining balance drops correctly.
6. Save the change and leave the audit log intact.

## Completed-event workflow

For a completed event such as CPB:

- keep the event available for reference
- review baker balances without reopening patron reminders
- use `Reports` for historical results
- use `Operations` for baker-payment closeout only

## Do not change patron totals

Do not change patron registration totals when updating a baker payment. Baker commitments belong to Operations closeout, not to registration income.
